############
###Usage####
1. Mode
2. Attack Type
3. Attack Damage
4. Stat effect
5. Stat target
6. Attack Name

* indicates a situationally meaningless parameter. For example, "Ember" has a stat effect of '*' because it has no stat effect where 'Leer' would have
an attack damage of '*' because it does not deal damage.

################
####Mode Key####
1 = attack
21 = stat mod(self)
22 = stat mod(point)

################
##Effects Key###
+ = step up one stat level
- = step down one stat level
